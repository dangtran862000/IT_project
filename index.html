CAREER PLANS

Overview
Instead of our team members having differences in the Ideal Job and Career path, we are similar to owning a great passion and a distinct plan. All members of our team decide to choose careers related to science and technology in general and information technology in particular. The careers that our team has chosen for their ideal jobs all need solid knowledge and technical skills as well as soft skills in an IT industry. Besides, we all want to practice and work in leading technology companies in Vietnam as well as around the world.
Common elements
We have a clear strategy to gain individual goals. Our goals come from passion without end with Information Technology. We also try to practice and learn more in our IT area. In common, all of our team members have a goal of completing and graduating a bachelor of information technology with a high score throughout the course. In addition, most of us focus on learning more short-term knowledge courses on extended skills to supplement the knowledge base as well as practical experience in the minor of each specific profession in the information technology industry.
Differences
Our ideal Jobs are not similar to each other. We have to learn different curriculums such as programming languages, algorithms. However, in the information technology industry is divided into many different fields. Although we have the same goal of stepping into life with the information technology industry, for each of our groups, we have our own fields including data scientist, web programmer, software engineer, and IT security office.
